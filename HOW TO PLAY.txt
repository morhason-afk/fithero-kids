FitHero Kids - Exercise Game

TO PLAY:
1. Install Node.js from https://nodejs.org (if not already installed).
2. Double-click:
   - start-game.command (Mac)
   - start-game.bat (Windows)
3. When the game starts, your browser will open to http://localhost:3002
   (If it doesn't, open that address in your browser.)
4. Allow camera access when the game asks (needed for some challenges).

Have fun!
